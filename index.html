.WHY.

Why music matters to all of us?

It starts long before we choose our own playlists. It’s the danzón our grandparents played in the background, the classics on the car radio on the way to school, the first time MTV showed us a song that felt like ours, like Los Fabulosos Cadillacs opening a door to a world we hadn’t met yet.

Music is a collage of inherited sounds, cultural roots, chance discoveries, heartbreak anthems, and songs we replayed until they became part of our personality.

We are made of the music that raised us, the music we chose later, and the music that found us at the exact moment we needed it.
